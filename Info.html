Statement

Jamie Young is an audio/visual artist based in Glasgow. Young's work comes from a fascination of the history of the universe, the evolution of life on Earth and the emergence of spiritual and religious practices. His work attempts to engage viewers with that which is within and beyond the limits of human perception by bringing astronomical objects and phenomena, notions of time, and encounters with various living organisms into a space before the viewer.
