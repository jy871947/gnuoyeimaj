Interzones

A "ly" (light-year), is a unit of distance in astronomy, specifically the distance light travels in one year as experienced on Earth. Bortle Class refers to the Bortle Scale, a nine-level numerical scale that measures the night sky's local brightness, ranging from Class 1, representing the darkest skies on the planet, through to Class 9, denoting areas of excessive light pollution/brightness. Integration refers to the duration over which a sensor collects and accumulates incoming light or signal. An unprocessed, single exposure has been included to highlight the differences between the pre- and post- processed images.
